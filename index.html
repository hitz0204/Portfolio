ABOUT ME

I am a creative passionate about the intersection between humans and technology. Whether designing a seamless user interface that guides users effortlessly through a site or exploring the unknown through creative coding, I am driven by the potential of technology to enhance human experience.
My curiosity and hunger to learn have been key to my growth as a creative throughout my studies. I thrive in exploring new fields, strategies, and processes, which allows me to shift perspectives and challenge conventions. I believe in combining techniques from various disciplines to create something unique and impactful.
Currently, I am seeking an internship where I can apply my skills, deepen my knowledge, and collaborate with a team to make meaningful contributions.

DISTANCE BASED CODED POSTERS

Description

Over the course of decades, the poster shaped the way information was communicated in public spaces. In an increasingly commercialized and digital world, the poster now faces new challenges. This experimental work explores the design of code-based and distance-responsive animated posters in order to illustrate a possible future for poster design.

CHRISTOPH RUMPF REDESIGN

Description

Between youth culture and high fashion lies the redesign of the namesake brand by Austrian designer Christoph Rumpf. The goal of the redesign was to close the visual and functional gap between the Instagram page and the shop. Through detailed analysis of personas, user journeys, and user stories, a usage concept was developed that prioritizes the needs of customers and communicates a clear brand identity.

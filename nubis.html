NUBIS DAS TRIERER NACHHALTIGKEITSNETZ

DESCRIPTION

Between youth culture and high fashion lies the redesign of the namesake brand by Austrian designer Christoph Rumpf. The goal of the redesign was to close the visual and functional gap between the Instagram page and the shop. Through detailed analysis of personas, user journeys, and user stories, a usage concept was developed that prioritizes the needs of customers and communicates a clear brand identity.

YEAR

2022

Course

Hypermedia

PROBLEM

The starting point of the redesign was both Christoph Rumf’s Instagram presence and his online shop. A clear contrast in visual quality became apparent: while the Instagram page stood out through high-quality product and editorial imagery, the online shop appeared comparatively underdeveloped. Due to a lack of information, the shop often felt untrustworthy and unprofessional, which in turn made the high price point seem unjustified. The brand’s ethos was barely reflected in the shop’s design, resulting in an unclear and weakly communicated brand identity.

IDEA

The fusion of youth culture and high fashion is communicated through unconventional design strategies. A bold element that remains permanently centered within the viewport breaks with established conventions and emphasizes the brand’s youthful and confident character. Scribbles and paper clips are used to create a sense of familiarity, evoking the feeling of personally marking products in a catalogue. At the same time, these elements reference mood boards commonly found backstage at fashion runways, bridging spontaneity with the creative processes of high fashion.
